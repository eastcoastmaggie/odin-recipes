Gingerdoodle Cookies

delicious soft ginger cookies

Best ginger cookies for your holiday celebrations. Or enjoy this all year round. Cookies are rolled is sugar for some additional sweetness

  • 1/2 cup butter softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cut packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon cloves
  • 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar for rolling

  1. Prehead the oven to 350 degrees

  2. Cream butter and sugar together until fluffy. Mix in eggs, vanilla and molasses. Whip on high for 2 minutes.

  3. Sift dry ingredients. Stir into butter mixture.

  4. Form balls and roll in sugar to coat. Place on lined baking sheet.

  5. Bake 7-9 minutes. Cookies will still be soft; let cool on sheet for 5-10 minutes.
